Frequently Asked Questions

Do Shopify Scripts stop working after June 30, 2026?
Shopify states that Shopify Scripts will sunset on June 30, 2026, and existing Scripts stop functioning after that date. This toolkit is designed to help teams prepare before late-stage pressure creates unnecessary risk.
What exactly is included in the toolkit?
The bundle includes the 46-slide strategic playbook for your Shopify Functions migration, a ready-to-use execution tracker (Google Sheets), a printable QA testing matrix, the Weekly Triage agenda, plus developer-ready copy-paste boilerplates and an AI prompt library.
Is this for Shopify Plus teams?
Yes. The product is designed primarily for Shopify Plus merchants, agencies, and developers managing Script-to-Function migration workflows.
Does this help with build vs buy decisions?
Yes. The workflow is structured to support clearer replacement decisions instead of treating every Script the same way.
Does this include QA guidance?
Yes. It includes a QA testing matrix and rollout-oriented validation structure.
Is this a course?
No. It is a practical toolkit and a complete Shopify Functions migration operating system.
Can agencies use this across clients?
Yes. It is suitable for repeatable client migration workflows.
